Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

  • Cook Time: 40 minutes
  • Serve: 4 Person

Tender chicken breasts filled with a delightful combination of spinach and creamy feta cheese, baked to perfection for a satisfying and flavorful meal.

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Toothpicks (for securing)

How to Cook Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and let it cool.
Butterfly the chicken breasts by slicing horizontally through the center, but not cutting all the way through. Open the chicken breasts and season them with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
Spread the cooked spinach evenly on one side of each chicken breast and sprinkle feta cheese on top. Fold the other side of the chicken breast over the filling and secure with toothpicks.
Place the stuffed chicken breasts in a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through. Remove the toothpicks before serving. Serve the chicken breasts with your choice of side dishes.
