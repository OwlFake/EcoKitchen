Roasted Beet and Goat Cheese Salad

Roasted Beet and Goat Cheese Salad

  • Cook Time: 1 Hour
  • Serve: 6 Person

An earthy and vibrant salad featuring roasted beets, creamy goat cheese, crisp greens, and a tangy vinaigrette, creating a perfect harmony of flavors and textures.

  • 4 medium beets, trimmed and peeled
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens.
  • 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to Cook Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Wrap each beet individually in aluminum foil and place them on a baking sheet.
Roast the beets for 45-60 minutes or until they are tender when pierced with a knife. Remove the beets from the oven and let them cool. Once cooled, cut them into wedges or cubes.
In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to make the vinaigrette. In a large salad bowl, combine the roasted beets, mixed salad greens, crumbled goat cheese, and chopped nuts.
Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to combine. Serve the roasted beet and goat cheese salad as a refreshing and nutritious meal.
