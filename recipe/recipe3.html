Chickpea and Vegetable Curry

  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Serve: 4 Person

A comforting and aromatic curry featuring protein-packed chickpeas and a medley of colorful vegetables, simmered in a rich and flavorful blend of spices and coconut milk.

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

How to Cook Chickpea and Vegetable Curry

Heat coconut oil in a large pot or skillet over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and saut√© until onions are translucent.
Stir in curry powder, cumin, coriander, and turmeric, and cook for 1-2 minutes to release the flavors. Add diced tomatoes with their juices, coconut milk, and mixed vegetables. Stir well.
Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Cook for 25-30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
Add chickpeas and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Serve the curry over steamed rice or quinoa, garnished with fresh cilantro.
